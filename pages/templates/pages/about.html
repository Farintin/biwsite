About Us

Beyond Imagination World Modeling Agency

The mission of BIW MODELS Academy is to TRANSFORM THE LIVES of young African Aspiring/established model To educate, inform, motivate, train and prepare Black males/females , grooming them into exceptional individuals, role models and leaders.

CORE BELIEFS

BIW MODELS Academy believes that there is an abundance of talented young NIGERIA'S in our community.

BIW MODELS Academy will support and work with them to achieve personal, social and academic success.

BIW MODELS Academy is committed to identifying and working with young Aspiring models by providing them with co-curricular learning experiences to equip them with the skills necessary for their development .

PROGRAM EMPHASES

Coach, mentor, and direct African MODELS participants along paths of self-exploration, improvement, and development, resulting in discoveries of creativity and a desire to excel.

CURRICULUM

BIW MODELS Academy is for SERIOUS individuals only. We are looking for individuals who are committed to:

Academic improvement

Attending program sessions

Actively participating

Helping others

Learning new behaviors and life skills

And becoming the absolute best he can be!
